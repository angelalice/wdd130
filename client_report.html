FINAL REPORT

Reflecting on my experiences on the final project for web design and development

Reflections

Client Report

To determine what content to use for my site plan, I first identified my three main topics and how I could make this website as useful as possible for users, even though the web page is not as dynamic as most. I also looked at various other websites to see how they structured their web pages and how the flow of the content fit together. This helped me to determine how to structure my content and which pictures to use. My developer sits at my table, and she always comes to class, so we have no issues with communication. My developer met my expectations, and the next time I hire a team to do something for me, I will maintain the level of specificity I include in my site plan and wireframe so they know exactly how I want my site to look.

Lead Developer Report

I used Teams to communicate with my team. I sent frequent updates to keep them informed, and so they would feel comfortable reaching out to me for help. I had a hard time reaching my client and had to wait a long time to get the site plan and wireframe. It took me some time to understand how to use GitHub; however, once I understood how to use it, it was a fantastic tool that allowed for easy collaboration. I was able to get a link to the site and shared it with my client so she could tell me if she liked it or not.

Another one of my team leads didn’t send us the site plan for a while, but once I had it, I finished the child page. I didn’t run into any issues adding to the sites.

General Experience and Reflections

The most challenging part of this project was communicating with others and waiting for their responses. If the person doesn’t choose to respond or do their part of the assignment, there isn’t much you can do. I enjoyed being creative and using all the skills I worked on over the semester. If I could do this project again, I would be more assertive in asking people to do their part. I feel more confident working on real-world projects after this experience because I have developed better teamwork and communication skills.
